who we are

Compassionate Mommies International group was founded on April 15th, 2017 when our President, in person of Pastor Mrs Anuoluwapo Koleola came across a homeless boy who was roaming the street of Ladipo market where he had already joined a market gang of street boys. She put the boy in a care home and nurtures him from time to time. Thereafter, she engage other noble women from various walks of life to support the projects of the group. We are emphatic women who never overlook the needs of the vulnerable ones in the society.
