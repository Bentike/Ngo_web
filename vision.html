About Us

Compassionate mommies is a Nigerian registered non-profit organisation based in Canada. We are a team of “passionate” women working together to achieve one goal: Alleviating poverty in the world. We are totally convinced that changes can happen when we collectively converge to propel them. The changes become sustainable when we share our knowledge, insights, and ideas with others and facilitate partnerships that enforce a more remarkable change. Combating poverty is a continual discovery process; there’s no one-size-fits-all solution. Through collaboration, we can create a sustainable means to finally reduce the rate of poverty in every continent of the world. We are not perfect, but we believe changing the world starts with lending a helping hand. “It's not how much we give but how much love we put into giving.” ~ Mother Teresa

OUR MISSION

Compassionate mommies strive to provide basic amenities for vulnerable and poor people in society. We engage in charitable causes such as providing:

  1. Education,
  2. Healthcare,
  3. Humanitarian services,
  4. Social and basic amenities,
  5. Monthly food bank to feed the poor.

We reach out to these people and support them until they can live a dignified life and most importantly, provide for their own families.

OUR VISION

We have uniquely integrated our core values to provide a sturdy foundation for our organisation. These values have shaped our relationship in relating with our beneficiaries, contributors and team members. They are:

  • Integrity
    Of all our values, we prioritise this the most. We achieve this by imbibing the strongest ethical principles in demonstrating utmost excellence. We believe that transparency is a supporting pillar of any organisation.

  • Resilience
    We understand that every experience is unique, and things may go unplanned. Every team member is driven to believe in the best in all circumstances. We endeavour to achieve our goals at all costs.

  • Accountability
    Our team is committed to complete accountability of funds and resources entrusted to our care. We ensure every action we take is documented for reference purposes.

  • Commitment
    We are committed to researching and implementing the best strategies to alleviate poverty in Africa. We are also committed to reevaluating ourselves to ensure we deliver humanitarian assistance to the areas that it’s needed.

  • Equality
    We do not discriminate. We strongly believe that everyone is entitled to equal rights, no matter the circumstances. Residents of poor communities can benefit from our programs regardless of race, gender, religion or creed.
